# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to providing a respectful and professional environment for all participants.

All contributors, users, and maintainers are expected to:
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and feedback
- Refrain from harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Act in good faith and with consideration for others

Unacceptable behavior may result in removal of content or restriction of participation at the discretion of the project maintainer(s).

This Code of Conduct applies to all project-related spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

For concerns or reports, please contact the project maintainer(s) via GitHub.
